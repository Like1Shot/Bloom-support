# Privacy Policy

**Bloom - Period & Cycle Tracker**

*Last Updated: February 10, 2026*

## Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Bloom is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe your health data is deeply personal, and you should have complete control over it. This policy explains how we handle your information.

## Summary

- **Your data stays on your device** - We do not have servers that store your health information
- **No accounts required** - Use Bloom without creating an account
- **No data selling** - We never sell, rent, or share your personal health data
- **You're in control** - Export or delete your data anytime

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Health Data You Provide

Bloom allows you to track:
- Period start and end dates
- Flow intensity
- Symptoms (physical, emotional, energy-related)
- Mood entries
- Cycle predictions and patterns

**This data is stored locally on your device only.**

### 1.2 Optional Apple Health Integration

If you choose to enable Apple Health integration, Bloom can:
- Read menstrual cycle data from Apple Health
- Write your logged data to Apple Health

This sync happens directly between Bloom and Apple Health on your device. We do not receive or store this data on any external servers.

### 1.3 Optional iCloud Backup

If you enable iCloud backup:
- Your data is encrypted before leaving your device
- Backups are stored in your personal iCloud account
- Only you can access this data with your Apple ID
- We cannot access your iCloud backups

### 1.4 Analytics (Non-Personal)

We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics to improve the app:
- App crashes and performance metrics
- Feature usage patterns (not tied to health data)
- Device type and iOS version

This data cannot identify you or be linked to your health information.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

Your health data is used solely to:
- Display your cycle history and predictions
- Generate personalized insights and tips
- Send optional reminder notifications (processed on-device)
- Create medical reports for your healthcare provider (at your request)

We do **not** use your data to:
- Build advertising profiles
- Train machine learning models
- Share with third parties
- Make decisions that affect you outside the app

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Local Storage

All health data is stored using Apple's secure frameworks:
- SwiftData with encrypted local storage
- Keychain for sensitive preferences
- Data remains on your device unless you explicitly enable iCloud backup

### 3.2 Encryption

- iCloud backups use end-to-end encryption
- Your data is encrypted before transmission
- Only your devices with your Apple ID can decrypt the data

### 3.3 App Lock

Bloom offers biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID) and passcode protection to prevent unauthorized access to your data.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

### 4.1 Advertising

Bloom displays non-personalized ads via Google AdMob. These ads:
- Are contextual, not based on your health data
- Do not receive any of your period or symptom information
- May use device identifiers for fraud prevention

You can manage ad preferences in Settings > Privacy & Data.

### 4.2 Apple Services

- **Apple Health**: Optional integration you control
- **iCloud**: Optional encrypted backup you control
- **Push Notifications**: Processed locally, no health data transmitted

---

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### 5.1 Access Your Data

View all your stored data within the app at any time.

### 5.2 Export Your Data

Export your complete health history as:
- JSON format for data portability
- PDF medical report for healthcare providers

### 5.3 Delete Your Data

Delete all your data instantly from Settings > Privacy & Data > Delete All Data. This action is irreversible.

### 5.4 Control Permissions

Revoke any permission at any time:
- Apple Health access
- Notifications
- iCloud backup

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

Bloom is intended for users who menstruate, typically ages 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided data, please contact us.

---

## 7. International Users

Bloom stores data locally on your device, regardless of your location. If you enable iCloud backup, your data is stored according to Apple's iCloud terms and your regional settings.

## 10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights:
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of sale of personal information

**We do not sell your personal information.**

To exercise these rights, use the in-app data management features or contact us.

---

## 11. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

EU residents have rights including:
- Access to your data
- Rectification of inaccurate data
- Erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Data portability

Since your data is stored locally on your device, you maintain full control. Use the in-app export and delete features to exercise these rights.
